## USAGE

*__sudoku.txt__ file contains the starting grid in the following format:*

```
7 * * * * 4 8 * *
* * * * * 5 4 * *
* * 9 * * * 7 * *
4 * * * * * * 9 *
8 * 7 * * * * * *
* * * 6 1 * * * *
* 3 * * 5 * * * 1
* 1 * 2 * * * 7 5
* * * 1 4 3 * * *
```

*where \* represents an empty space.*
